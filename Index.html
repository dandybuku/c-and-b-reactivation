Insights & Recommendations

Double down

Multi-channel is the unlock

Practices that clicked both email and in-app convert at 48.8% — nearly 7× the 7.2% baseline. Build retargeting sequences that explicitly reinforce across both surfaces.

Opportunity

33 practices dropped at checkout

132 started a case, only 122 submitted. A same-day "finish your case" rescue email/push could recover 15–20 of these warm leads with minimal effort.

Problem

Email clicks are the bottleneck

51.8% open rate is strong, but only 4.0% click through — a 60-point gap. Subject lines work; the body CTA doesn't. Test a single-action email with one clear ask.

Problem

In-app CTR is very low at 2.0%

93.6% reach, 12.5% ever click. High visibility, low action. The ad may be appearing at low-intent moments. Test placement timing and social-proof-led creative.

Insight

Converters got fewer emails

Submitted practices averaged 5.8 emails vs 7.1 for non-converters. Volume is not the driver — engagement quality is. Heavy senders may flag low-intent audiences.

Insight

869 practices saw ads, never engaged

Even a 3-point lift in this unengaged segment adds ~26 conversions. A creative refresh with a concrete value prop ("first crown case in under 5 minutes") is worth A/B testing.

Open Questions

01

What's in these emails — single CTA or multiple?

The open-to-click gap suggests the email body may be creating decision fatigue. A single focused ask likely outperforms a multi-option layout.

02

Is there a conversion timing pattern?

Are conversions clustering after email 1 or email 4? Converters averaged fewer emails, suggesting early touchpoints matter most. Cadence optimization could be significant.

03

Where in the product does the in-app ad appear?

A 2% CTR with 93% reach suggests the ad may be surfacing at low-intent moments in the workflow. Placement timing and context could dramatically move this number.

04

Data integrity: 23 practices submitted without a case-started flag

These show case_submitted but has_case_started = 0. Worth validating — likely a tracking gap in the funnel instrumentation.

05

Is there a time-since-delivery dimension missing?

Knowing whether conversions cluster in days 1–3 vs 7–14 would meaningfully help optimize send cadence and the in-app ad suppression window post-conversion.
